# Project Frostpeak Game Design Document
**Version 1.0**

## Section 1: Game Summary & Overview
Project Frostpeak is an immersive tactical role-playing game set in the frozen ruins of the Northern kingdom of Frostpeak. The player assumes the role of a legendary pathfinder aiding the Frostpeak Resistance in reclaiming their home and restoring the lineage of King Arven from the occupying forces of the Cinder Vanguard.

## Section 2: Narrative Direction & Lore
The story centers around the aftermath of the Ember Siege of 842. The volcanic nation of Vulcana, led by Warlord Ignis, launched an elemental assault to seize control of Frostpeak's geothermal energy and rich sky-iron deposits. Today, the Cinder Vanguard guards the mines, while rangers launch guerrilla attacks from the Ash Pass to free the frozen city. Faction choice holds high consequences for gameplay and world state flags.

## Section 3: Art Style Direction
The visual identity of Project Frostpeak utilizes a Stylized Dark Fantasy theme. We utilize a high-contrast color palette, pairing warm oranges and elemental reds from the Cinder Vanguard's fire engines against deep icy blues and granite grays of the frozen Northern citadel ruins. Characters have sharp, hand-painted textures that highlight the gritty, cold environment.

## Section 4: NPC Archetypes
The game features two key starting NPCs:
- **NPC Eldrin**: Archmage of the Watchtower. Eldrin serves as a high-trust scholar and dialogue advisor. He speaks in a formal, slow manner and references ancient lore history to guide the player.
- **NPC Kaelen**: Commander of the Cinder Vanguard garrison. Kaelen is a pragmatic, cautious warrior. He offers high-risk quests and values faction standing and strength above all.

## Section 5: Level Design Suggestions
The starting layout centers on the Geothermal Vents zone. Players navigate a vertical cliffside path interspersed with active volcanic vents, sky-iron ore veins, and steam checkpoints. The level culminates at the monumental East Gate, which remains barricaded by Cinder Vanguard defenses.

## Section 6: Quest Hooks
The golden path includes the following quest lines:
- **Quest Hook 1**: Objective: Reclaim the Ash Pass border post. Players must clear guards and raise the Northern flag. Reward: 100 gold and 250 XP.
- **Quest Hook 2**: Objective: Mine 5 chunks of raw sky-iron ore from the cliffside veins. Reward: 150 gold and sky-iron gear.
